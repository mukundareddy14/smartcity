Updates of DELHI


Some of the major updates in Delhi city from the past 6 months are:

Metro timings changed

- The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) issued revised metro timings for Diwali, with services commencing from 6:00 a.m. on all lines except the Airport Express line¹.

Traffic Restrictions in Delhi city

- The Delhi government decided to implement the odd-even traffic restriction plan from November 13 to 20 to check air pollution levels, which have been in the 'severe' category for several days¹².

CM Arvind kejriwal's case High court decision

- The Delhi High Court stayed the summons to Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal's wife by the Enforcement Directorate in connection with a money laundering case related to an alleged Delhi excise policy scandal².

Anouncement by AAM AADMI PARTY

- The Aam Aadmi Party announced that it will hold a nationwide 'referendum' to ask people if Kejriwal should run the government from jail if he is arrested by the ED¹.

ANTI-SMOG tower Restarted

- The Delhi government restarted the anti-smog tower at Connaught Place, a day after the Supreme Court rebuked it for not functioning properly².

High court desicion on CHHATH POOJA

- The Delhi High Court dismissed a plea challenging the ban on Chhath Puja celebrations in public places due to the Covid-19 pandemic².

Ban ON Online TAXI

- The Delhi government banned the entry of app-based taxis from other cities in a bid to curb pollution, following the suggestion of the Supreme Court².
