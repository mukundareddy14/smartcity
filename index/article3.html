Al Hilal hand Mumbai fourth straight Champions League loss

NAVI MUMBAI: The 2023-24 AFC Champions League season continues to be a harsh journey for Mumbai City. The Islanders were handed their latest painful lesson at DY Patil Sports Stadium here on Monday, going down 2-0 to Saudi Pro League side Al Hilal, the competition's most decorated champions.

Maharashtra to get Anti Narcotics Task Force under IG-rank officer to curb drug menace


Asserting that Maharashtra police has a "zero tolerance" policy towards drugs and drug peddling, top officials on Thursday said that the state will soon have an Anti Narcotics Task Force headed by an Inspector-General rank officer to curb the menace, reported the PTI.

Five cyber police stations brought under crime branch

MUMBAI POLICE Commissioner Hemant Nagrale has brought all the newly formed cyber police stations, located in each of the five regions of the city, under the ambit of the Mumbai crime branch.
